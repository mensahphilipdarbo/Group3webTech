FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

Do I need an appointment to visit?

Walk-ins are welcome for general consultations, but booking an appointment guarantees a shorter waiting time and a dedicated slot with your preferred doctor.

Which insurance schemes do you accept?

We accept NHIS (National Health Insurance Scheme) and all major private health insurance plans including GLICO, Enterprise, and Nationwide.

How do I access my test results?

Lab results are available within 24–48 hours and can be collected at the clinic. Results can also be sent to your email upon request when you register.

Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

Yes. Please call our reception at least 24 hours in advance to cancel or reschedule. You can also send an email to our appointments team.
