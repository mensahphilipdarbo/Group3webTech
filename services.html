Our Medical Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions delivered by expert professionals, all under one roof.

All Departments

What We Specialise In

Search or browse all available services at USTED Clinic.

🩺
Out-Patient

General Consultations

Our general practitioners provide thorough diagnosis and management of a wide range of medical conditions, from routine check-ups to chronic disease monitoring.

  • Routine health examinations
  • Chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension)
  • Minor surgical procedures
  • Medical certificates & referrals
  • Travel medicine & vaccinations
🔬
Diagnostics

Laboratory & Diagnostics

Our fully equipped laboratory provides accurate, fast results for a comprehensive range of diagnostic tests to support clinical decision-making.

  • Full blood count & biochemistry panels
  • Malaria rapid diagnostic tests
  • HIV, hepatitis, and STI screening
  • Urinalysis & stool microscopy
  • X-ray & ultrasound imaging
💊
Pharmacy

Pharmacy Services

Our on-site pharmacy is stocked with a comprehensive range of medications, dispensed by licensed pharmacists who provide guidance on proper use.

  • Prescription & over-the-counter medications
  • Medication counselling & education
  • Drug interaction checks
  • Chronic disease medication supply
  • Vaccines & health supplements
🦷
Dental

Dental Care

Comprehensive dental services for patients of all ages delivered by qualified dentists using modern equipment and techniques.

  • Dental examinations & cleaning
  • Tooth fillings & extractions
  • Root canal treatment
  • Dentures & orthodontics
  • Cosmetic whitening procedures
🤰
Maternal Health

Maternal & Child Health

Dedicated maternal and child health services ensuring safe pregnancies, healthy deliveries, and healthy child development from birth to adolescence.

  • Antenatal care & monitoring
  • Safe delivery services
  • Postnatal care & breastfeeding support
  • Child immunisation & growth monitoring
  • Family planning counselling
🚑
Emergency

Emergency & Trauma Care

Our 24/7 Emergency Department is fully equipped to handle acute conditions, trauma, and life-threatening emergencies with rapid-response specialists.

  • 24/7 emergency response
  • Trauma management & wound care
  • Resuscitation & critical care
  • Emergency surgery support
  • Ambulance coordination
🧠
Specialist

Mental Health Services

Compassionate mental health support from trained counsellors and psychologists for individuals, couples, and families navigating emotional challenges.

  • Individual & group counselling
  • Stress & anxiety management
  • Depression screening & therapy
  • Substance abuse support
  • Referrals to psychiatric care
🏋️
Rehab

Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation

Personalised physiotherapy programmes to help patients recover mobility, strength, and function after injury, surgery, or chronic musculoskeletal conditions.

  • Musculoskeletal physiotherapy
  • Post-surgical rehabilitation
  • Sports injury treatment
  • Neurological rehabilitation
  • Hydrotherapy & electrotherapy
👁️
Specialist

Eye Care (Ophthalmology)

Comprehensive eye care services including vision testing, prescription eyewear consultations, and treatment of common and complex eye conditions.

  • Vision & refraction testing
  • Prescription glasses & contacts
  • Glaucoma screening
  • Diabetic eye examinations
  • Cataract management & referral
